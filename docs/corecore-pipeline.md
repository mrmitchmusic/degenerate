# Corecore Pipeline

This workflow is for making Corecore-style videos that support your song using a mix of:

- weird or funny internet references
- emotionally charged quotes
- royalty-free image/video assets
- text overlays
- your own song clips

## Recommended Agent Flow

1. Strategy Lead defines the emotional direction.
2. Corecore Researcher finds reference material, quotes, themes, and oddities.
3. Asset Sourcing Agent finds royalty-free replacements and supporting visuals.
4. Script Writer and Visual Director turn those materials into a vertical narrative.
5. Edit Agent builds the sequence and timing.
6. Publishing Agent packages captions and posting notes.

## Good Corecore Inputs

The best source material tends to fall into these buckets:

- lonely or awkward interview lines
- funny but bleak observations
- dreamlike public-domain footage
- surveillance-style urban clips
- abstract textures
- nostalgic domestic footage
- youth-culture fragments
- headlines, forum fragments, or text-based confessions
- royalty-free clips that feel uncanny, sterile, intimate, or emotionally distant

## Output Standard

Every final video package should include:

- the emotional thesis of the video
- the selected song clip
- the quote or text fragments to use
- the actual assets to use
- the source references and replacements
- the exact edit order
