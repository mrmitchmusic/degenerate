## Review Questions

- Which concept had the clearest first 2 seconds?
- Which concept created the most comments that implied identity or relatability?
- Which concept seemed easiest for other creators to imitate?
- Did any post teach people how to use the sound?
- Which clip timing felt strongest?

## Decision Rules

- Scale concepts that get clear audience recognition and early reuse signals
- Revise concepts that have decent engagement but unclear reuse behavior
- Kill concepts that are confusing, high-effort, or only work when posted by the artist
